CNA Certification Requirements in California

To become a Certified Nursing Assistant in California and start your CNA training in Pomona, you must meet these requirements:

  • ✅ Be at least 16 years of age (with parent/guardian consent if under 18)
  • ✅ Have a valid Social Security Number (SSN) or ITIN
  • ✅ Pass a health screening and TB test
  • ✅ Clear a Live Scan background check
  • ✅ Complete a CDPH-approved training program (like ours!)
  • ✅ Pass the state certification exam

Frequently Asked Questions About CNA Training in Pomona

How much do CNA classes cost in Pomona, CA?

Our CNA program costs $1,995, which includes all materials, textbooks, and exam fees. However, most students qualify for WIOA funding that covers 100% of the cost. We also offer payment plans for those who need them.

How long does it take to become a CNA in California?

With our accelerated program, you can become a certified nursing assistant in just 5-6 weeks. This includes 160 hours of training (60 hours theory + 100 hours clinical) as required by the California Department of Public Health.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes! We have partnerships with healthcare facilities throughout Pomona, Los Angeles County, and the Inland Empire. Our career services team helps with resume writing, interview preparation, and job placement.
